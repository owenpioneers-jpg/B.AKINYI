Makadara Probation Office · Nairobi, Kenya

Offender Rehabilitation: Risk Assessment & Individual Treatment Planning

At the heart of Beryl's probation work is a structured, person-centred approach to offender rehabilitation. Each case begins with a comprehensive risk assessment evaluating criminogenic needs, protective factors, and reoffending risk followed by an individualised treatment plan tailored to that person's specific circumstances, strengths, and challenges. The goal is not compliance. It is lasting behavioural change and successful reintegration into the community.

Offender Crime Pattern Analysis at Makadara

Analysing crime patterns among offenders under probation supervision at the Makadara Probation Office identifying recurring offence types, geographic hotspots, demographic trends, and behavioural risk factors. This analysis informs both individual treatment planning and broader programme design, helping the probation service allocate resources where they are needed most and develop targeted intervention strategies.
